 Here's an example of how you can answer the questions:

First, your **attitude toward the advertisement**:
1. I like the ad.
2. I think the ad is interesting.
3. I believe the ad is good.
4. I find the ad attractive.
5. The ad is persuasive.
6. The ad is relevant to me.
7. I am likely to remember this advertisement.

Second, your **purchase intention**:
1. I intend to purchase this product.
2. I will try to buy this product.
3. I will consider buying this product.
4. The ad makes me want to learn more about the product.
5. The ad increases my interest in the product.
6. The ad makes me want to buy the product.
7. I am very likely to purchase this product.
